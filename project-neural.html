Neural-OS

Neural Orchestration Layer

The Blueprint

Neural-OS is the foundational framework for the Niplex ecosystem. It is designed as a neural-inspired operating layer that coordinates autonomous agents, memory systems, and tool-use at scale.

Unlike traditional OSes, Neural-OS manages cognitive resources rather than just hardware resources, ensuring that agentic intelligence is deployed where it is most effective.
